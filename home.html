Left-leaning Anura Kumara Dissanayake wins 2024 election

Left-leaning politician Anura Kumara Dissanayake won Sri Lanka's September 2024 presidential election after a historic second round of counting.

With his victory, Sri Lanka has for the first time a government led by a strong left-wing ideology. He promised good governance, anti-corruption measures and economic recovery.
